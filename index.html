About

Ana García Caicedo is a filmmaker based in Ontario, Canada. She decided to pursue filmmaking at Fanshawe College after graduating from Seneca Polytechnic with an H.B.Tech. in Software Development.

Since 2023, she has directed several short narrative films, documentaries, and experimental films. Her work explores themes of obsession, technology, and isolation, while also drawing inspiration from the horror media she grew up with. In 2025, her experimental short ":(" was screened at the Forest City Film Festival.
